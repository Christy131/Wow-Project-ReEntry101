ReEntry Guides:

You can download an electronic version for free through Here, or you may call(217) 300-5150 or request by email @reentry@educationjustice.net. Or you can request by mail to Education Justice Project 1001 S Wright St. Champaign, IL 61820.

Social Security and Disability:

You can visit the Social Security Administration website Here, or you can call them at 1-800-722-1213.
