About Us

Just a brief description, we were created by succussful returned citizens, who came out of prison and successfully re-entered society. They know what it is like to adjust to the world, and wanted to make the transitioning for others easier to deal with. They are amazing men and women who are willing to bend over backwards for their fellow brother/sister.

Congrats to this month's Shout Out:

Julia B

Each month we give a shout out to a returned citizen letting you know we see you and how proud we are of you.

Came home on her birthday in 2022, she struggled to adjust with life outside of prison. Julia quickly remembered a re-entry course she took while in prison, she ended up doing a few searches on Google came across our page. Julia found where she could apply for food stamps, find a place to stay, and find community. Congrats Julia, for 6 years sober.
