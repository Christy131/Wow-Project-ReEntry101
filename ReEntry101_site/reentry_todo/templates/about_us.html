About Us

Reentry 101 – is the ultimate online resource for the justice-impacted. 1 out of 5 people in the US has been to prison or impacted by the justice system. Our platform is designed to provide a supportive and inclusive community where justice-impacted individuals can connect with one another and access a wealth of resources to help them navigate the challenges of reentry. At Reentry 101, we understand the transition from prison to the "free world" can be daunting. Offering a one-stop shop for information about, housing, shelter, education, and day-to-day living needs that they struggle with is what we try to address. Our community of like-minded individuals offers a safe and welcoming space where you can connect with others who have shared similar experiences and challenges. Through our forums, you can find support, advice, and guidance from people who have been through it all. At Reentry 101, our mission is to help you build a better future and avoid the cycle of recidivism. Join our community today and take the first step towards a brighter tomorrow.
